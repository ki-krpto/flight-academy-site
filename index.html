Do you look to the skies when you hear the faint sound of an engine from above? Have you dreamed about the freedom of flight? We here at FFA know exactly how you feel! Fallbrook Flight Academy offers tailored, individualized training that will help you gain the skills & confidence you need to become a safe, competent pilot. Whether you want to become a Commercial Pilot or are just an aviation enthusiast, we have a program to help you reach your aviation goals!
